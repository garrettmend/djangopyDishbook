Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon (zest + 3 tbsp juice)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup pasta water (reserved)
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water; drain.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Sear in olive oil over medium-high until browned and cooked through; remove.
  3. Lower heat; add butter and garlic, cook 30 seconds. Stir in lemon zest and juice.
  4. Add pasta, chicken, and some reserved water; toss. Off heat, add Parmesan until glossy. Adjust with more water if needed.
  5. Finish with parsley, season to taste, and serve immediately.
